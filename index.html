Xu Luo

Xu Luo is a third-year master student of computer and science at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) under the supervision of Zenglin Xu. In 2021, he interned at Huawei Cloud under the guidance of Longhui Wei. Before that, he received B.E. degree in mathematics from Xiamen University in 2019. Currently, his research interests lie in Few-Shot Learning and Representation Learning.
